12 Studio • 100s of Creatives • 1 Night in Durham

When?

April 16th, 5:30 - 9 p.m.

Why?

We have more fun when we join together. Students meet students. Studios meet creatives. Creatives meet future employers. Community meets you.

How?

Register for free, and take a self-guided tour with our handy maps. Carpool, take a bus, or walk to two areas of Durham: American Tobacco Campus and Golden Belt.

Who?

Designers, students, teachers, marketers, videographers, programmers, illustrators, photographers, writers, and other creatives.

AIGA Raleigh Studio Tour | Durham

Once again AIGA Raleigh invites creatives to a Studio Tour. This year our annual self-guided tour takes us to design, creative, and marketing agencies in Durham.

On April 16, creatives from all over the Triangle will descend on Durham. And you’ll see more people and places in one night than you could see in a year of AIGA Raleigh community meetings.
